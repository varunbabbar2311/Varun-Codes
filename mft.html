B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering with Specialization in Mainframe Technology in academic collaboration with IBM

This B.Tech degree program prepares the students on mainframe technologies and increases the employability of the student. This is the foundation for mainframe application development. This course starts with the history and basics of the Mainframe, Mainframe Hardware, Mainframe networking, Virtualization, z/OS, Parallel Sysplex. The course also covers the application development modules and open source technologies.
